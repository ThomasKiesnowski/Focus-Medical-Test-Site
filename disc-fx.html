System Design

Less Disruption.
Faster Recovery.

3.0mm Working Portal

Dramatically smaller access than traditional open discectomy. Less tissue disruption, less bleeding, faster recovery for patients.

Three Treatment Modalities

Nucleus debulking, RF ablation via Trigger-Flex®, and annulus modulation — all addressable through one system.

Minimum Annulotomy

Patented approach minimizes the annular defect size to reduce risk of herniation recurrence — a key limitation of traditional techniques.

Disc-FX Mini — Cervical Variant

Extends the same minimally invasive philosophy to cervical disc herniations. Separate system purpose-built for the cervical spine.

Outpatient Capable

Procedure designed for office, ASC, or hospital. Same-day discharge supports efficient patient throughput.

FDA Cleared

Proven technology from Elliquence, a leader in minimally invasive RF and discectomy technology for the spine.
