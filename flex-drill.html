Flex Drill™

Access Tool

Flexible drill for precision vertebral body access, creating a controlled cavity optimized for cement fill distribution.

V-STEADY™ Pairing

High Viscosity

Pair with V-STEADY high-viscosity cement for targeted single-level kyphoplasty where precise flow control is critical.

For multi-level procedures, pair with V-FAST low-viscosity cement for efficient cement delivery across consecutive vertebral levels.

Product Features

Engineered for Precision Kyphoplasty Access

Flexible Drill Shaft

Flexible design navigates vertebral body anatomy for optimal access angles that rigid drills cannot achieve.

Controlled Cavity Creation

Creates a precisely shaped cavity within the vertebral body, optimizing the volume and geometry for uniform cement distribution.

G21 Cement System Compatible

Designed to pair seamlessly with G21 V-STEADY and V-FAST PMMA cement systems for a complete kyphoplasty workflow.

FDA Cleared

Meets rigorous FDA regulatory standards for kyphoplasty access devices, ensuring safety and performance in clinical use.

Optimal Cement Fill

Cavity geometry is engineered to promote even cement distribution, reducing the risk of extravasation and improving structural support.

Minimally Invasive

Designed for percutaneous kyphoplasty procedures, supporting minimally invasive access with reduced tissue disruption and faster recovery.
