Product Features

Designed for Controlled, Confident Delivery

Two Viscosity Options

V-STEADY (high viscosity) and V-FAST (low viscosity) cover the full range of clinical vertebroplasty scenarios — one system for every case type.

Superior Radiopacity

High barium sulfate concentration delivers outstanding radioscopic contrast for real-time fluoroscopic visualization during injection.

Low Polymerization Temperature

Reduced exothermic reaction during polymerization minimizes thermal shock risk to adjacent vertebral tissue and neural structures.

V-MIX™ Preparation System

PicoMix™ V mixer ensures safe, consistent cement preparation. Luer-lock syringe connection eliminates manual handling and reduces contamination risk.

Extended Injection Window

9+ minute injection window gives the surgeon time for confident, controlled cement delivery at each level — without time pressure or premature setting.

FDA Approved & ISO 13485

Both V-STEADY and V-FAST formulations meet rigorous international regulatory standards — FDA approved and CE marked for vertebral consolidation procedures.
