Key Differentiator

No Screws.
No Fusion. No Overnight Stay.

ScrewLES® takes a fundamentally different approach to lumbar spinal stenosis — achieving stable interspinous fixation without pedicle screws, laminectomy, or general anesthesia. A less-than-2-inch incision and ~1-hour procedure means your patients go home the same day and return to activity faster.

Product Features

Less Invasive. Equally Effective.

No Pedicle Screws Required

Interspinous fixation achieves stability without pedicle screw placement — reducing operative risk and eliminating screw-related complications entirely.

~1-Hour Procedure

Short operative time supports high case volume in an ASC or office-based setting. Patients return home the same day with minimal recovery requirements.

Less Than 2" Incision

Minimally invasive approach preserves musculature and minimizes soft tissue disruption — less pain, faster healing, and fewer post-op complications.

Zero Device Failures (10 Years)

FDA cleared since 2010 with zero reported device failures. The implant's structural integrity has been proven across a decade of real-world clinical use.

Full T1–S1 Coverage

FDA cleared for use at all thoracolumbar and lumbosacral levels from T1 to S1 — broad applicability for the full range of lumbar spinal stenosis cases.

Local or General Anesthesia

Flexible anesthesia options accommodate a wider patient population — including patients who are poor candidates for general anesthesia in traditional fusion procedures.
