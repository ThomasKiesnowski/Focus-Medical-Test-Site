Key Differentiator

"One Marker.
Every Modality."

Standard skin markers force teams to switch markers between imaging sessions — or accept poor visibility on certain modalities. IZI Multi-Modality Markers are engineered from hydrogel to appear clearly and consistently on CT, MRI (all sequences including fat-suppression), PET, nuclear medicine, and X-ray simultaneously.

No modality switching. No re-registration between scans. No painful rigid-marker indentation. One marker handles the entire image-guided workflow — from initial planning through final confirmation.

Visible on All 5 Modalities
CT, MRI, PET, nuclear medicine, and X-ray — including fat-suppressed MRI sequences where many markers fail.
Soft Hydrogel — Patient Comfort
Eliminates the painful skin indentation caused by rigid plastic or metallic markers. Patients remain comfortable throughout extended multi-scan procedures.
2mm Center Hole — Direct Pen Marking
The central 2mm hole fits a standard pen tip, allowing direct skin marking through the marker for precise patient registration reference.
Product Features

Built for Precision Imaging Workflows

Multi-Modality Visibility

CT, MRI, PET, nuclear medicine, X-ray. Visible across all five major imaging modalities — no modality switching or re-marking required during the procedure.

Artifact-Free Imaging

Hydrogel construction prevents the distracting image artifacts that rigid or metallic markers produce — keeping scan reads clean and diagnostic quality high.

Soft & Comfortable

Unlike rigid markers, the soft hydrogel design eliminates painful skin indentation. Patients remain comfortable throughout extended multi-scan procedures.

Pen-Tip Registration

The 2mm center hole is sized precisely to fit a standard pen tip. Direct patient marking through the center provides a reliable reference for precise registration and re-registration.

Secure Adhesion

Medical-grade adhesive backing adheres securely to skin throughout the full duration of the procedure — even with patient repositioning between imaging sessions.

Ready-to-Use Kit

50 sterile, single-use markers per jar. Ready to open and apply with no prep. Available in multiple sizes to meet varied clinical requirements.

Clinical Applications

Where IZI Markers Perform

Navigational Surgery — Patient registration and re-registration for image-guided navigation systems
CT / MRI-Guided Interventions — Reliable surface reference markers during cross-sectional imaging procedures
PET Imaging — Visible on PET without interfering with radiotracer uptake visualization
Biopsy Guidance — Surface reference marking for percutaneous biopsy and ablation targeting
Radiation Therapy Planning — Consistent, artifact-free positioning markers for treatment planning scans
IR Reference Marking — Interventional radiology surface landmarking prior to fluoroscopic procedures
