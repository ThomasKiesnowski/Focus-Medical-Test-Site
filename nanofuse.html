Key Differentiator

Dual-Mechanism Fusion.
Three Clinical Forms.

Most graft substitutes work by one mechanism. NanoFUSE combines 45S5 bioactive ceramic with DBM to deliver both osteoinduction (stimulating new bone formation) and osteoconduction (providing a scaffold for bone growth) — giving surgeons a biologically superior scaffold in the form that fits their procedure best.

NanoPutty™

Moldable putty form — ideal for filling irregular voids and coating implant surfaces. Stays in place during implant insertion.

NanoStrip™

Strip form for intervertebral and intraosseous applications. Easy to cut and shape for precise placement in interbody or lateral approaches.

NanoBead™

Bead form for packing bone defects and voids. Uniform size enables consistent biological delivery across the treatment site.

Product Features

Biologically Superior Bone Fusion

45S5 Bioactive Ceramic

45S5 bioglass is one of the most studied bioactive ceramics in orthopedic surgery. It forms a direct chemical bond with bone, integrating at the molecular level for superior osteointegration.

Osteoinductive + Osteoconductive

DBM component delivers osteoinductive growth factors that stimulate new bone formation. Bioactive ceramic scaffold provides the osteoconductive matrix. Both mechanisms working together.

Three Delivery Forms

NanoPutty, NanoStrip, and NanoBead give surgeons the right form factor for every indication — from coating implant surfaces to packing bone voids to intervertebral placement.

Broad Surgical Applications

Validated for use in spine (anterior and posterior fusion), orthopedic reconstruction, and neurosurgery applications — a single product line across multiple departments.

Fully Resorbable

NanoFUSE products gradually resorb as new bone forms — no permanent foreign material left behind. The scaffold is replaced by the patient's own regenerated bone tissue.

Biocompatible & Sterilized

All NanoFUSE products are biocompatible, sterilized, and ready for immediate use. No reconstitution required — open, apply, and proceed with the procedure.
