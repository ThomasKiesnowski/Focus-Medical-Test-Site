System Design

Engineered for Fusion.
Not Just Fixation.

3D-Printed Titanium

Textured surface directly promotes osteoconduction for reliable bony fusion across the sacroiliac joint.

Dual Differential Pitch Thread

Creates controlled compression across the joint as the implant is advanced — engineered for fusion, not just fixation.

Anti-Backout Design

Anti-migratory external threads and castellated insertion feature prevent toggle and implant migration after placement.

Lateral Approach

Single lateral entry provides access to the full SI joint. Fenestration allows allograft delivery directly across the joint.

Precise Sizing

9.5mm, 11.5mm, and 13.5mm diameters accommodate different anatomy. Tapered design maximizes cortical bone purchase.

Imaging-Confirmed Placement

Lateral, inlet, and outlet fluoroscopic views confirm screw positioning at every step of the procedure.
