Optimally-sized tracking beads provide real-time flow visualization under fluoroscopy. See exactly where cement is going — throughout the entire injection.

18-Minute Working Window

Extended working time gives you confidence in multilevel procedures. No rushing. Complete your injection at the pace the case demands — not the cement's timeline.

Ready in under one minute. Efficient preparation means less OR or procedure room time — and a smoother workflow for your staff on every case.

Optimal Interdigitation

Medium viscosity is specifically formulated for efficient trabecular interdigitation in the vertebral body — achieving strong mechanical fixation across varied bone quality.

Variable-sized barium sulfate particles provide excellent radioscopic visibility. Combined with Insite™ tracking, you always have a clear picture of cement distribution.

Indications for Use

Built for Pathological Vertebral Fractures

Vertefix® MV is indicated for fixation of pathological fractures of the vertebral body resulting from the conditions below — in both vertebroplasty and kyphoplasty settings.

Osteoporosis & Osteopenia

Vertebral compression fractures from low bone mineral density — the most common indication for vertebral augmentation.

Vertebral Hemangioma

Benign vascular lesions of the vertebral body causing pain or structural compromise requiring stabilization.

Metastatic Cancer

Malignant lesions resulting in painful vertebral fractures. Vertebral augmentation provides rapid pain relief and structural restoration for oncology patients.

Multiple Myeloma

Plasma cell malignancy with high rate of vertebral involvement. Vertebroplasty with Vertefix® MV restores vertebral body integrity and relieves compression fracture pain.
